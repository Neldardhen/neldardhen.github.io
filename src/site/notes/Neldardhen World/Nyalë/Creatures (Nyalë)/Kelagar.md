# Kelagar
Beast from [[Neldardhen World/Nyalë/Creatures (Nyalë)/Creatures from Nyalë\|Nyalë]]
![blue-blood-deer.jpg|200](/img/user/Images/Creatures/blue-blood-deer.jpg)
Despite its appearance of a deer, the Kelagar is a vicious carnivor.

### Fundamentals
Level 5

|  Mind   |    Body     | Spirit       |
| :-----: | :---------: | ------------ |
|    4    |      8      | 8            |
| _focus_ | _endurance_ | _resilience_ |
|   12    |     24      | 24           |
**Types** : warm blooded, fur, terrestrial
**Speeds** : Walking **14m**, Swimming **2m**, Flying **0m**
**Traits** :
- **Sunlight Sensitivity** : The Kelagar is not able to fullfil its need to dream if it as been exposed to at least 1h of sunlight during the last 24h.
- **Cloak in Shadows** : The Kelagar benefits from 1d8 natural armour and veil.
### Attacks and Abilities
**Ram**
**2d6** cost 1 *endurance*
Single target range **14m**
Damages :
- **3d6** Piercing
Move up to its speed in a straight line and attacks one target.

**Kick**
**2d6** cost 1 *endurance*
Single target range **2m**
Damages :
- **2d6** Bludgeoning
Kick a target in front or directely behind.

**Grim Feast**
Recharges on a **5**
**4d6** cost 3 *endurance*
Single target range **2m**
Damages :
- **3d4** Piercing
- **2d4** Fear
The Kelagar start eating its target alive. The target takes 3d4 piercing damage, every ally of the target that can see this happening takes 2d4 fear damage (cannot be dodge, only resisted)
